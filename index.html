Cross Trade Shipping

Our global cross trade teams can help you move cargo from any origin to any destination.

  • At Cargosmart we understand that sometimes our customers need to move cargo to and from countries in which they themselves aren’t based. That’s why we’ve established our local cross trades team, who can help with queries and bookings outside of the country from where your business operates.
